Admissions

Start your journey with EduPlus today

Your Future Starts Here

At EduPlus, we're committed to making quality education accessible to all qualified students. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, helping you take the first step toward achieving your academic and career goals.

Whether you're a recent high school graduate, a working professional looking to upskill, or an international student seeking world-class education, we have a place for you.

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to join our community

1. Explore Programs

Browse our course catalog to find the program that aligns with your goals. Attend an information session or schedule a campus tour to learn more.

Admission Requirements

Our admission criteria vary by program level to ensure students are prepared for the academic rigor of their chosen course of study.

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5 (on a 4.0 scale)
  • Official high school transcripts
  • SAT/ACT scores (optional for 2023-2024)
  • Personal statement (500 words)
  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 (on a 4.0 scale)
  • Official transcripts from all colleges attended
  • GRE/GMAT scores (program dependent)
  • Statement of purpose
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Professional resume/CV
  • All standard program requirements
  • Proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS)
  • Evaluation of foreign transcripts
  • Financial documentation
  • Copy of passport
  • Official transcripts from all colleges attended
  • Minimum GPA of 2.0 for associate, 2.5 for bachelor's
  • Course descriptions for transfer evaluation
  • Good standing at previous institution
