About EduPlus

Our story, mission, and vision for the future of education

Who We Are

Founded in 1995, EduPlus has grown from a small local institution to an internationally recognized leader in innovative education. Our commitment to excellence has remained unchanged through the years.

We believe education should be transformative, accessible, and tailored to meet the needs of a rapidly changing world. Our approach combines rigorous academics with practical, real-world applications.

Accredited by the Global Education Board
Recognized as a Top 10 Institution for 5 consecutive years
98% student satisfaction rate
28 Years of Excellence

Our Mission

To empower individuals through transformative education that fosters intellectual growth, professional development, and personal fulfillment. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment that prepares students to meet the challenges of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in innovative education, recognized for developing future-ready graduates who drive positive change in their communities and industries worldwide.

Our History

Milestones in our journey of educational excellence

1995

Foundation

EduPlus was founded with just three classrooms and a vision to provide accessible, quality education to the local community.

2002

First Accreditation

Received accreditation from the Regional Education Board, validating our academic standards and quality.

2010

International Expansion

Launched our first international programs, welcoming students from over 20 countries.

2015

Digital Transformation

Pioneered online learning platforms, making our courses accessible to students worldwide.

2020

Global Recognition

Ranked among the top 10 educational institutions in the Global Education Index.

2023

Future Ready

Launched our Future Skills initiative, preparing students for emerging technologies and industries.
